making medical processes faster and safer.

It is aiding us by making hospital information more accessible and easy to understand.

Helps with care of critically ill patients and helps us with therapy in the medical field both physical and mental health.

It benefits us by making it cheaper and more accessible to get health care while making it more advanced and safe for us to use.
